Refund & Return Policy

Refund Policy

Refunds are accepted under the following conditions:

  • Refunds will be processed through the original mode of payment.
  • Refunds will be processed within 10 to 45 business days depending on the issuing bank of the credit card.


Return Policy

Returns are accepted within 7 days of receipt only if:

  • The product is incorrect or damaged
  • The delivery packaging has not been opened or tampered with

In such cases:

  • Notify us with your order details.
  • Return the item using a registered courier service.
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging.

We will issue a refund or send a replacement if all conditions are met. Shipping & handling fees are non-refundable. We reserve the right to refuse any returned shipments if the product has been used or tampered with.
