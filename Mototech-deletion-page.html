Mototech App – Account Deletion

This page explains how Mototech app users can request deletion of their account and personal data according to our data protection practices.

How to Request Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your Mototech account and all associated data by contacting us using one of the methods below:

  • Email: help@mototech.ae
  • Include your registered phone number or email used in the app.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Once we receive your request, we will permanently delete:

  • Your user profile
  • Saved addresses
  • Order history (unless required for legal or financial compliance)
  • All personal details stored in our system

Processing Time

Deletion requests are processed within 7–30 days. You will receive a confirmation once your data has been removed.

Data We May Retain

Some information may be retained as required by applicable laws for accounting, fraud prevention, or legal compliance. This information will not be used for any other purpose.

Need help? Contact our support team at help@mototech.ae for assistance.
